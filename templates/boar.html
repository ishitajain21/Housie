How to play

  1. First, make sure you have the tickets in your last name in front of you. Make sure the ID is also printed.
  2. Next, click Start! You will see the board, a table to the left, a Search ID and a Next Number button.
  3. Click the Next button and you will see the next number on the right in the table. Keep clicking and you will see the previous numbers too.
  4. If someone has won something, type the ID in the text box on the left of SearchID button.
  5. You will see their ticket right under the Back button.
  6. Yow will also see the table for the convenience of the other players.
  7. Once you have confirmed that the player has indeed won the prize, type the amount of money won in the first row.
  8. Type the last name, first name and what they won in the following rows.
  9. Press submit once done.
  10. You should see the prize and name in the table.
  11. Once game is done or when you want to see the money won, click ENDGAME.
  12. Enjoy! Best wishes, Ishita.

Color Key

In a table means that a number has been called.
In a ticket means that the number in a ticket has been highlighted.
